Fun and weird facts about worms

Worms don’t have lungs. They breathe through their skin, which has to stay moist for oxygen to pass through.

Worms don’t have eyes, but they can sense light. In fact, they avoid it—sunlight can dry them out and even kill them.

Earthworms can move objects that are 10 to 50 times their own weight—relative to their size, that’s like a human dragging a car.

Some worms can regenerate parts of their bodies, like their tail. But if you cut one in half, you usually don’t get two worms—just one dead and one injured.
